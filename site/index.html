The Space

More Than a Shop

The Wild Dandelion Collective is 2,000 square feet of curated warmth, tucked behind two storefront doors in Longmont, Colorado. Part boutique, part micro salon, part gathering place for creators who believe beautiful things should be shared.

Step inside and you will find hand-picked goods alongside professional beauty services, seasonal art, and the kind of atmosphere that makes you lose track of time in the best way.
