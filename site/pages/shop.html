The Shop

Curated with Intention

Every product on these shelves was chosen because we use it, love it, and stand behind it. Shop in person for the full experience, or browse online through our partner stores.

Hair Care

Davines

Born in Parma, Italy, Davines creates professional hair care that balances beauty with sustainability. Their products are formulated with care for your hair and the planet, using high-quality ingredients sourced with respect for the people and places that grow them.

As an authorized Davines salon, we carry the full range: shampoos, conditioners, treatments, and styling products chosen specifically for the textures and needs we see every day in our chairs.

Shop Davines Online
Clean Beauty

Seint Beauty

Seint takes the overwhelm out of makeup. Their IIID Foundation system simplifies your routine into a single compact, custom-built for your unique skin tones. No complicated steps, no ten-product morning routine.

We love Seint because it aligns with everything we believe about beauty: it should enhance what is already there, not cover it up. Clean ingredients, personalized palettes, and results that feel effortless.

Explore Seint Beauty
Seasonal & Special

Curated Goods

The curated section of our shop changes with the seasons. Candles from small-batch makers, jewelry from local artisans, vintage finds that caught our eye, home goods that make your space feel a little more like you.

We do not keep a standing online inventory for these items because half the joy is discovering them in person. Stop in, see what is new, and walk out with something you were not expecting to find.
