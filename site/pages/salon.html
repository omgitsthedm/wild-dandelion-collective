The Micro Salon

Intimate by Design

Our salon is not trying to be big. It is four chairs, good music, warm light, and the kind of appointments that feel like catching up with a friend who happens to be incredible with hair.

The Experience

Just Four Chairs, Real Conversation

Most salons are designed for volume. Ours is designed for connection. Every appointment gets the time it deserves, with a stylist who actually listens and a space that feels nothing like a production line.

You will hear vinyl on the speakers, smell something good burning near the front, and leave feeling like the best version of yourself. That is the whole idea.

Services

What We Do Behind the Chair

From lived-in color to sharp cuts, bridal styling to nourishing treatments. Every service is tailored to you.

Cuts & Styling

Precision cuts for all textures, blowouts that last, and the kind of styling that makes you feel like yourself on your best day.

Color

Balayage, highlights, vivids, and full color transformations. Lived-in, dimensional color that grows out beautifully.

Treatments

Deep conditioning, Davines rituals, scalp treatments, and restorative sessions for hair that needs a little extra love.

Bridal & Events

Wedding-day styling, special occasion looks, and the kind of hair that photographs beautifully from every angle.

Consultations

Not sure what you want? Start with a conversation. We will figure it out together, no pressure, no upselling.

Extensions

Natural-looking extensions for volume, length, or both. Custom blended and applied with care for your hair's health.
