Our Story

From Nomadic Stylist to Collective Dreamer

Ashley Dania DeMarco spent years moving through salon chairs, pop-up studios, and kitchen-table conversations before she found the space that matched her vision. The Wild Dandelion Collective was never supposed to be just a salon or just a shop. It was always meant to be a feeling.

The Vision

A Verdant Creative Space

The word "collective" is deliberate. This is not one person's store. It is a gathering of stylists, makers, and curators who share a belief that beauty should feel personal, goods should be chosen with care, and a great space should welcome everyone who walks through the door.

The 2,000-square-foot studio with two storefront doors is designed for lingering. The kind of place where you came for a haircut and left with a candle, a new favorite product, and a conversation you did not expect to have.

Community First

What "Collective" Really Means

A collective is a group of people working toward something shared. Here, that means stylists who set their own pace, retail that supports small makers, and a space that belongs to the community as much as it belongs to us.

Every chair in our micro salon is run by an independent artist. The shop shelves feature products we actually use, believe in, and stand behind. And the doors are always open for anyone who wants to come in, browse, breathe, and feel something good.

Our Philosophy

Beauty, Wellness, Art, Community

These four pillars hold up everything we do. Not as marketing words, but as the actual filter through which every decision gets made.

Beauty

Hair, skin, and self-care done with intention. Never rushed, never cookie-cutter. Your look should feel like you.

Wellness

Clean products, sustainable brands, and the kind of environment that actually lowers your heart rate when you walk in.

Art

Creativity is not a side dish here. From hair transformations to seasonal installations, everything in this space is made with an artist's eye.

Community

Independent stylists, local makers, and neighbors who become regulars. A collective is nothing without the people who fill it.

Come Experience It for Yourself

Words on a screen only go so far. Walk through the doors and feel the difference.
